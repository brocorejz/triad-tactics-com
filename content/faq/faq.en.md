# FAQ

Times shown on this page are displayed in your local time

---

## 1. When do games take place?

Games are held every Sunday

Slotting for the first episode starts at 15:45 CET

A total of 3 episodes are played, each lasting 1–1.5 hours

---

## 2. Where can I get the server password?

The password is revealed by the administrator on the website during slotting

Order of access:

1. Squads and priority players
2. Solo players

If you are a solo player — you must sign up for slotting on the website in advance, otherwise you will not receive the password

The password is released gradually:

- first to squads and priority players
- then to solo players

---

## 3. When does the mission appear on the website?

Missions are usually published closer to the end of the work week

---

## 4. How can I download mods in advance?

The server and password for downloading mods appear together with the mission, usually closer to the end of the week

The server remains available until the start of the games

---

## 5. There were no available slots, how can I join the games?

One episode lasts about 1–1.5 hours. After it ends, the next one starts

Often not all players stay for the 2nd or 3rd episodes, so free slots may appear there

You can also join server tests, which are announced in advance on Discord

---

## 6. We have a squad, how can we get guaranteed slots?

At the moment:

- join as solo players

If you:

- consistently gather a group of at least 6 players
- regularly attend games for several weeks

— contact the administration to discuss getting priority status

---

## 7. Registration says no game found — how to fix it?

Make sure that:

- your Steam profile is public
- your game list is visible

This is required for the bot to verify that you own `Arma Reforger`

You can check this in your Steam privacy settings

---

## 8. How do I take a slot as a priority player?

On the mission slotting board:

1. Find a free slot for priority players
2. Click “take”
3. Make sure your nickname appears in the selected slot
